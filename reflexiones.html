About This Book

Reflections, your wonderful pocket book, will help you reconsider, understand, and find solutions to the difficult situations or events that may arise along your path, deepening your learning and your strength. It will also offer you guidance toward caring for your physical and emotional health, reconsidering ill-founded addictions and preventing stress and despair.

The author highlights the importance of the manifestation of joy, giving value to every occasion in life and giving you control of your thoughts. She emphasizes gratitude, the greatness of willpower, the strengthening of discipline, the improvement of habits, and the carrying out of excellence through a positive attitude. Reflections is an invitation to delve into the improvement of your ideas, to calm your mind, and to lead a life of fullness.

Meet the Author

Maria del Rocio Beach

Maria del Rocio Beach is an author and entrepreneur based in Hawaii. With a unique background combining experience in business and the teaching of English, Maria brings a multifaceted perspective to her writing. Her second book, Reflections, represents five years of personal transformation and spiritual healing after being diagnosed with an autoimmune disease.

Through her lived experience and her teachings, Maria shares practical tools for emotional growth, personal discipline, gratitude, and excellence in life. Her messages transcend languages and cultures, inviting readers around the world to discover the inner strength that resides in every human being.
