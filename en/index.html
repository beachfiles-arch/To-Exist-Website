About This Book

To exist is a lesson of life, from a mental and physical standpoint, after the diagnosis of an autoimmune disease. Through this magnificent book, you will learn to know your body, to love yourself, to listen to your inner self, and to put to test different strategies. It also invites you to foster discipline, happiness and energy, in order to establish a new lifestyle beyond what you expect.

To exist will be your best ally as a source of motivational inspiration to overcome complex and debilitating circumstances that you will encounter at some point in your life. A book that will fill you with love, strength, energy and light that you will need to transform into a new version of yourself.

Meet the Author

Maria del Rocio Medina Anguiano

I am Maria del Rocio, born in Mexico City. I relocated to California in 2016. In 2019, I began to suffer from ulcerative colitis. The struggle to find information about the disease and its cures led me on a journey that found its way into the pages of this book.

Today I am recovered, and the steps that led me back to healthfulness I am compelled to share.
